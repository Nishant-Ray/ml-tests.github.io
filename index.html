Learn

at the Game Developement Club

Learn

On Fridays at lunch, we offer detailed lessons about programming in C#, the Unity game engine, and game development in general. We also cover a wide variety of other topics, including 3D Modeling and Machine Learning.

Make

At our Friday afterschool sessions, members are challenged to implement a small game mechanic to improve their coding and game development skills. We also host game jams for members to develop full games.

Share

On Monday at lunch, members can play or watch a game selected by our officers and discuss what game design decisions the developers made. Members can also show off the games they made at game showcases hosted by the club.
